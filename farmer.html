PROJECT: HYDROPONICS MARKET AND EXPERIENCE CENTRE │ CATEGORY: COMMERCIAL │ LOCATION: PAN INDIA (Prototype) │ CLIENT: HYDROBLOOM

In this era of rapid urbanistaion we are relying on the third world regions for our food creating a huge disparity. Farming is no more a sought after profession. Even kitchen gardens are fading away due to space constraints difficulty in providing resources. Hydroponics can be a solution for this. Hydroponics is a fairly new method in the area of farming. With hydroponics you can always have a small kitchen garden right away in your balcony or any place at home. Not a lot of people are aware of it, and people who knows are sceptical about the process. They are doubtfull about the set-up cost, yield, maintainance requirements and practicality of it. And if someone opts for large scale hydroponics farm, they don't yet have platform to sell their products apart from the current web of flawed distribution system. Thus came up with the Idea of a marketplace for hydroponics products, people can directly market their products here, also showcasing the technology to the public. The experience centre includes a presentation platform and a few car kitchens which serve the food directly from farm -hydroponics of course- to table. This was a prototype design which can be replicated all over the country.
