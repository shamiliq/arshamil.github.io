PROJECT: DENTAL CLINIC │ CATEGORY: HEALTHCARE │ LOCATION: MANJERI, MALAPPURAM, KERALA │ CLIENT: DR. FINIYA

This dental clinic is located in the healthcare capital of the district, Manjeri. It has space for 2 practitioners, a receptionist, a waiting lounge and a private room for the chief doctor. The exterior was designed with a rugged style with smoked wood and stone as elements, while the interiors are minimal and light themed.
